Placement Cell

The placement cell is a student initiative which has evolved from the internship cell and it is comprised entirely of students. The main aim of the cell is to provide placements & internships to students of the college enrolled in various courses.

It helps students to identify their strengths, interests, and areas of improvement. Partnering with industry experts leads to better understanding and ensures that academic programs remain relevant and competitive.
